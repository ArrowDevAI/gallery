About Me

An interest in problem-solving and technical curiosity led to an early career as an Avionics professional. In this role, I performed system installations for navigation, communication, flight guidance, and cabin systems. Over time, new-age avionics replaced bulky hardware with compact cabinets filled with circuit cards and software. As the work shifted toward software installation and manipulation, my interest naturally followed.

After seven years in Avionics, I shifted my focus to software development. My Full-Stack educational experience provided exposure to both theory and practical application. I am excited to say that software development is as challenging, puzzling, and rewarding as I had hoped. I am comfortable manipulating the command line, console, and terminal to troubleshoot. I have grasped the nuances of communication between the front and back ends. I have built a strong foundational understanding of navigating the stack and I am prepared for the projects and challenges ahead.
